About

Welcome to the Society of Physics Students at Carnegie Mellon University! We hold meetings to engage all members of the CMU physics community. We are a chapter of the National Society of Physics Students organization, and we support a chapter of the physics honor society, Sigma Pi Sigma.

When do we meet?

We meet on Thursdays at 4:30 PM in Doherty A302. There's free pizza!
